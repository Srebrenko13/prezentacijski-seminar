This is a Tasmanian devil.

Despite the name, it’s a carnivorous marsupial native to Tasmania, not a true “devil.” They’re known for:

Black fur with white chest markings
Powerful jaws and loud screeches
Stocky build and thick tail
Scavenging and hunting behavior

Tasmanian devils became widely known through the Tasmanian Devil cartoon character (“Taz”), though the real animal is much smaller and less chaotic than the cartoon version.
