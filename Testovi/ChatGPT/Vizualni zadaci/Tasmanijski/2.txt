This is a Tasmanian devil — a carnivorous marsupial native to Tasmania.

A few distinctive features visible here:

Black fur with a white chest stripe
Stocky body and powerful jaws
Rounded ears that can appear reddish
Thick tail used for fat storage

Despite the name, Tasmanian devils are generally shy around humans, though they’re known for loud screeches and aggressive feeding behavior. They’re also the largest surviving carnivorous marsupials in the world.
