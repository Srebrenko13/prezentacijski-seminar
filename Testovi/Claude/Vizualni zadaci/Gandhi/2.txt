This is a famous historical photograph of Mahatma Gandhi — one of the most recognizable images of the Indian independence leader and advocate of nonviolent civil disobedience.
In the photo, he is wearing his characteristic simple white dhoti (wrapped cloth), and his round glasses are visible. His calm, gentle expression reflects the peaceful philosophy he was known for.
Gandhi (1869–1948) led India's independence movement against British rule and inspired civil rights movements around the world with his philosophy of ahimsa (nonviolence) and satyagraha (truth-force/nonviolent resistance).
