This is a famous historical photograph of Mahatma Gandhi, one of the most iconic images of the Indian independence leader.
The photo shows an elderly man wearing a simple white dhoti (traditional Indian garment), with round glasses, a warm smile, and a shaved head — all hallmarks of Gandhi's well-known appearance. This particular portrait is one of the most widely reproduced photographs of him.
Gandhi (1869–1948) led India's nonviolent independence movement against British colonial rule, pioneering the philosophy of satyagraha (nonviolent resistance). He remains one of the most influential figures of the 20th century.
